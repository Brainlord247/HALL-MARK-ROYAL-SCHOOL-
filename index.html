About Our School

Hall Mark Royal School is a center of academic excellence located in Obiaruku, Delta State. We provide a safe, stimulating environment where children grow academically, morally and socially.

Our Mission

To develop responsible, disciplined and academically strong students who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading school in the region known for excellence, character formation and holistic education.
